Bacon Sandwich

Description

A sandwich containing bacon.

Ingredients

  • 5 slices good quality smoked streaky bacon, or 3 back
  • 2 slices soft white bread
  • Butter
  • Ketchup, if you must

Method

  1. Fry the bacon to taste, needs a bit of colour at least
  2. While frying the bacon, butter the bread
  3. When the bacon is ready put it on one slice of buttered bread, adding sauce if you want
  4. Place the other slice of bread on top
  5. Serve with beverage of choice, milky coffee for me
