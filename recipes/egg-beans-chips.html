Egg, beans and chips

Description

The clue is in the name.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of baked beans
  • 2 eggs
  • Maybe 3 medium potatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Butter

Method

  1. Gently warm up the beans in small saucepan
  2. Heat up a decent amount of oil in a largish frying pan
  3. Slice the potoatoes into approx 1cm thick discs
  4. Chop the discs into chips, or don't, up to you
  5. Fry them until golden brown, turning a couple of times as you go
  6. In another pan, heat some oil and a little butter for the eggs
  7. Fry the eggs to your liking
  8. Put it all on a plate and serve with a beverage of your choice, preferably tea
