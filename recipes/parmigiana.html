Parmigiana

Description

An old Italian comfort food classic, using aubergine, tomatoes, mozarella and, of course, parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of whole peeled plum tomatoes
  • 2 large aubergines
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 1 stick of celery
  • 2 balls of mozarella
  • Olive oil
  • Block of parmesan
  • Some basil leaves

Method

  1. Heat a decent amount of oil in largish pan
  2. Dice up the carrot, onion and celery, nice and small
  3. Brown in the pan, and when they're half way, add the garlic, crushed
  4. When it's all nicely browned, deglaze with some juice from one of the tomato cans
  5. Tip both cans of tomatoes in and simmer down, breaking up the fruit with a spoon
  6. Season to taste
  7. Once satisfied with the sauce, turn off the heat and let it sit for a bit
  8. Warm up a grill pan to high heat
  9. Slice the aubergines lengthways, approx .75 cm thick
  10. Salt the slices and brush with oil
  11. Grill them until soft and golden brown
  12. Heat up the oven to 200ish C
  13. Grate a bowl of parmesan and break up the mozarella balls into smallish pieces
  14. Wash and pick the basil leaves
  15. Layer up the dish, sauce, aubergine, parmesan, mozarella until used up, saving extra cheese for the top
  16. Try not to make it too wet, and add in basil leaves and generous amounts of black pepper as you go
  17. Put in the oven until golden brown on top, approx 25 minutes
  18. Serve with bread and salad
